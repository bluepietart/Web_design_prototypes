About Us

Enigma Of An Artist

We are a buch of kids crazed by the idea of art. How to draw art, make art, create art etc. We keep on searching on ways to make art as it is a passion to replicate what we seen with our eyes by our on hands. In a way creating things that alredy exist in a 3D world, and replacting the art 'mostly drawing' on a 2D plane gives us the pleasure of creating something and to be recognised for it. Its akin to being GOD in a way. Though we pioneer ways to make art and explore artistic natures we still do not understand the end goal of why we create things. What does it give us, what value does it hold. All unanswered questions but even now unanswered. A struggle to find answers. That is the true pursuit of a true artist.
